### Week 25 — Sensors as “feelers”: reed switch buzzer

Use: `../../templates/weekly-session-template.md`

#### Objectives

2) Discuss sensors in machines (limit switch, float) (5–10 min)
3) Notebook; Stop Here (10–15 min)

#### Safety
